General FAQs
1. What is Dobu Martial Arts?
Dobu Martial Arts is a training center focused on traditional martial arts techniques, emphasizing discipline, strength, and self-defense skills.
2. What are the training hours?
Our training hours vary depending on the program. Generally, classes are available from 6 AM to 9 PM daily. Please check our schedule page for specific class timings.
3. Do I need prior martial arts experience to join?
No, our classes are designed for all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. Instructors will guide you based on your skill level.
4. Is there an age requirement to join Dobu?
We offer programs for various age groups, starting from age 5 for youth classes. Adult classes are open to individuals 16 and older.
5. What should I wear to the classes?
Comfortable workout attire is recommended for beginners. Advanced students are encouraged to wear the traditional martial arts uniform available for purchase.
6. How can I sign up for a membership?
You can sign up for a membership through our website's Membership page or in person at our training center. Different plans are available based on your needs.
7. Do you offer any special discounts?
Yes, we offer discounts for students, veterans, and families. Please check our Membership page or contact us for more details on eligibility.
